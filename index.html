Introduction

Welcome to BD Gold Calculator — a free tool built for Bangladeshi users to estimate gold jewelry prices using traditional units (Vori, Ana, Roti, Point) with real-time purity and tax calculations.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android application. We are committed to transparency and to protecting your privacy. Please read this document carefully.

Plain-language summary: We do not collect your personal data. The only data-related activity involves Google AdMob, which may collect anonymised advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads.

Google AdMob & Advertising

To keep BD Gold Calculator free, we use Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial advertisements. Ads are shown at natural transition points (after a calculation is completed) to minimise accidental taps.

Google AdMob may collect and use the following to serve relevant ads:

  • Google Advertising ID (GAID) — a resettable, anonymous device identifier.
  • General device information (model, OS version, screen size).
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (not GPS).
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks) for billing and fraud prevention.
You may opt out of personalised advertising at any time via Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID or by enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalisation."

Google's own privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy and the AdMob Partner Policies.

3

Internet Access & In-App Browser

The app requests internet permission for two purposes:

  • Quick Check feature: Opens an in-app WebView to load live gold market rates from Google Search or BAJUS (Bangladesh Jewellers Association). We do not log, intercept, or store any data transmitted during these browsing sessions. The WebView is subject to the privacy policy of the site you visit.
  • Ad delivery: As described in Section 2.

The core gold calculation engine works entirely offline — no internet connection is required for Vori/Ana/Roti/Point calculations, purity conversion, VAT, or making charge computation.

Children's Privacy

BD Gold Calculator is a general-purpose financial utility app intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

Google AdMob is configured to serve non-personalised ads in contexts where child users may be present, in compliance with Google's Families Policy and COPPA requirements.

If you believe a child has inadvertently provided personal information through our app, please contact us immediately using the details in Section 8 so we can take appropriate action.

Your Rights & Choices

Because we do not collect personal data, most data-subject rights (access, rectification, deletion) do not apply directly to us. However, you have the following controls:

  • Ad preferences: Reset or delete your Google Advertising ID in Android Settings at any time.

  • Uninstall: Removing the app immediately stops all data collection by AdMob related to your device.
